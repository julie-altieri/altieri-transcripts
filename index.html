About Altieri Transcripts

Altieri Transcripts provides certified Ontario court transcripts for law firms, courts, and self-represented clients. All work is completed by an Authorized Court Transcriptionist in Ontario.

We follow the standards set by the Ministry of the Attorney General to support accuracy, confidentiality, and timely delivery of certified transcripts.

View the official registration record at Arkley Transcriptionist Registry.

Services & Rates

All Authorized Court Transcriptionists are mandated to charge the following rates for the production of transcripts, as per Ontario Regulation 145/22 (amending Ontario Regulation 94/14) under the Administration of Justice Act.
