FAQ: Court transcription in Ontario

Answers from Julie Altieri, Authorized Court Transcriptionist in Ontario. This section helps you find details fast and improves search for “Julie Altieri transcripts” and “court transcription Ontario.”

Who is the transcriptionist?

I am Julie Altieri. I am an authorized court transcriptionist in Ontario. I produce certified court transcripts for law firms, courts, and the public.

What services do you provide?

Certified Ontario court transcripts. Criminal, family, civil, small claims, and POA. Standard, expedited, and daily turnaround.

How do I order a transcript?

You have two options. Use the official order form linked above. Or use the Contact section to share your case details. I will reply with a quote and the next steps.

What information do you need to start?

Case name, court file number, courthouse, courtroom or judge, and date or range of dates. This allows the courthouse to locate audio.

What are the rates in Ontario?

Rates follow Ontario Regulation 145/22. Standard $6.30 per page. Expedited $8.80 per page. Daily $11.75 per page. HST applies.

How do you estimate pages and cost?

One hour of audio usually produces 30 to 40 pages. Use the estimator above for a quick range before HST and before the $25 minimum.

Do you handle appeals?

Yes. Provide the appeal number if available. If not available, enter TBC. I will guide you through what the court requires.

How do I receive the transcript?

Delivery is electronic by default in certified PDF. If you require print, say so in your request.

How do I pay?

Invoices are sent by email. Payment options include e-transfer and credit card. Details are provided with the quote.

Privacy and confidentiality

I follow the Ministry standards for security and confidentiality. I handle sensitive material with care and only for the purpose of producing the transcript.

Where do I read official guidance?

For more information about court transcript procedures, visit the Ontario Court Transcript FAQ.
